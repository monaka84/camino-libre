Privacy Policy

How Camino Libre handles personal information, cookies, web analytics, and inquiry data.

1. Introduction

Camino Libre (hereinafter "this site") sets out below the handling of information collected when you use our services. By using this site, you are deemed to have agreed to this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2-1. Access Information

This site uses Google Analytics (provided by Google LLC) to analyze access. The following information is automatically collected:

  • IP address (anonymized by Google Analytics)
  • Browser type, OS, and device information
  • Referring URL, pages viewed, and time on page
  • Approximate location (country / city level)

Cookies are used for this purpose. No personally identifying information is included.

2-2. Information Collected via Inquiries

When you contact us by email, we collect your email address, name, and the content of your inquiry. These are used solely for replying to you and for record keeping.

2-3. Server Logs

This site uses a hosting service (Cloudflare Pages). The service may temporarily record server logs such as IP addresses and access times.

3. Purpose of Use

The collected information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Understanding site usage and improving content and usability
  • Responding to inquiries
  • Addressing unauthorized access, spam, and similar issues

4. Disclosure to Third Parties

Collected information will not be provided to third parties except with your consent or in response to a legal request.

However, in connection with the external services mentioned in Section 2 (Google Analytics, Google Fonts, Cloudflare Pages, etc.), information is processed under each provider's respective privacy policy.

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy?hl=en
  • Cloudflare Privacy Policy: https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/

5. Cookies and Opt-Out

This site uses cookies for analytics. If you do not wish to allow cookies, you can disable them in your browser settings.

If you wish to opt out of Google Analytics tracking, you can use the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on provided by Google.

6. External Links and Embedded Content

This site uses Google Fonts for display optimization. This sends your IP address and browser information to Google.

Articles may contain links to external sites such as Google Maps. For information on how those sites handle information, please refer to each site's privacy policy.
