Monaka
Traveler · Rider · Former Costa Rica Resident

After living in Costa Rica, I became drawn to travel and daily life across Latin America. In Japan, I keep making motorcycle trips around the country.

"Camino Libre" means "free road" in Spanish. Here I record my travels and life in Costa Rica and other Latin American countries, my journey learning Spanish, and my motorcycle trips.

In Latin America, while visiting tourist spots, I've been drawn most to the scenes embedded in everyday life — the back alleys, small eateries, the small exchanges with locals. In Japan, I ride. Coastal roads, mountain passes, straight roads that seem to go on forever — there are sights you only catch by riding through them yourself.
